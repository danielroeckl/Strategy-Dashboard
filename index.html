How the strategic score works
Score =
( Benefit × 0.5  +  Innovation × 0.5 )0.72 × 15.81
√Effort × 0.5  +  Cost × 0.03  +  0.3

Customer benefit

4 sliders (0–10). Top 2 count double.

Innovation

10 types (0–10). Top 4 count double.

Effort

XS–XL. Higher effort lowers the score.

Cost

XS–XL. Higher cost lowers the score.

High ≥ 45 — strong value relative to effort, prioritise
Medium 25–44 — solid project, review scope or ambition
Low < 25 — low impact or high cost, reconsider
